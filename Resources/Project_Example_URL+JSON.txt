		"rendered": "<h3>Cloud-based Platform for Wi-Fi Device Management</h3>\n<p>Principal Interaction Designer in charge of UX for the company&#8217;s 6 interactive products:</p>\n<ul>\n<li>Personal home/business wi-fi manager (iOS + Android) (now <a href=\"https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.cloudmondo.consumer\">blazeX</a>)</li>\n<li>Tablet-based Network Operation Center for cloud-managed wi-fi devices (iOS + Android) (now <a href=\"https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.cloudmondo.pocketNOQ\">blazeNOQ</a>)</li>\n<li>Web-based Network Operation Center for cloud-managed wi-fi devices (Web)</li>\n<li>Cloud-managed wi-fi device provisioning app  (Android)</li>\n</ul>\n<p>I was in charge of:</p>\n<ul>\n<li>Understanding requirements from customers and stakeholders</li>\n<li>Translating business requirements and platform constrains into user-oriented and user-friendly interfaces</li>\n<li>Understanding the intricacies of the platform (easy set-up of cloud-based agent-managed &#8216;routers&#8217; by both technicians and end-users)</li>\n<li>Defining the workflows, screens, processes and interactions for the user interface</li>\n<li>Coordinating with the development team to get designs into production pipeline</li>\n<li>User-oriented and functionality QA control</li>\n<li>Management of the app&#8217;s online access points (Google Play Store /
